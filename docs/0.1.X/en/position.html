position

property

position properties are defined as left , center , right

left : align position to the left.

center : align position to the center.

right : align position to the right.

example

hello world! left

hello world! center

hello world! right

p class="left"hello world! leftp
p class="center"hello world! centerp
p class="rightello world! rightp
